**CREATE A NEW FILE**

> touch <file_name>.txt

or 

> nano <file_name>.txt

or

> mousepad <file_name>.txt

or

> gedit <file_name>.txt

**Overwrite a File**

> echo 'Overwrite' > <file_name>.txt

**Append to a File**  - Used while Scripting

> echo 'Append' >> <file_name>.txt
